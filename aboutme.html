Get to know me

Here are a few things about me!

Ever since high school, I've been passionate about design and art. I used to draw everyday, after a while I learned to crochet and sew.

During that time, I learned to code (which I really liked!), but I always wanted to find a connection with art and the word computer. It wasn't easy until I realized I was already doing it. I draw something in Illustrator and then tried to turn it into a tapestry. After that I discovered more about fiber design and ui/ux design.

I have to say, I'm a beginner in this word, but really excited to learn more and more every day. Even this website is made by me!
